# 0015 Comparative Mapping Families and Render Bundles

## Summary

Add the direct-audification and token-stream families plus the required render and provenance bundles.

## Scope

- Define the direct-audification mapping family.
- Define the feature-token mapping family.
- Define the render bundle for finalized objects, including stems, synchronized visual outputs, legends, and provenance records.
- Preserve comparability across mapping families.

## Non-Goals

- Benchmark evaluation.
- Optimization of mappings or prompts.

## Global Constraints

- All committed outputs must remain formal, concise, precise, and limited to essential content.
- Mapping families must remain documented, reproducible, and comparable.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The additional mapping families have stable contracts aligned with the core sonification policy.
- Finalized render bundles contain the required audio, provenance, and explanatory artifacts.
- Cross-family comparisons can be made without hidden normalization changes.
